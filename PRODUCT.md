# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Players of a browser arcade survival game, mostly using quick sessions where combat readability and fast menu recognition matter more than decorative UI.

## Product Purpose

Brainrot Swarm is a mobile-first, canvas-based bullet-hell survival game. Success means players can read threats, rewards, progression, and navigation at a glance while the game keeps its chunky arcade personality.

## Brand Personality

Arcade readable, playful, bold.

## Anti-references

Avoid low-contrast HUD elements, thin outlines, muted gray labels, generic dark sci-fi panels, over-polished SaaS controls, and visual noise that hides combat state.

## Design Principles

- Match the existing orange arcade shell, chunky buttons, strong outlines, and bright reward colors.
- Combat clarity wins: player, enemy, boss, pickup, and camera state must be readable immediately.
- Use high contrast and shape differences, not color alone, for important gameplay markers.
- Keep HUD controls compact and stable so they do not distract from play.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Prioritize high contrast, color-blind-safe marker shapes, readable text, and minimal non-essential motion. Respect the existing mobile/desktop HUD split.

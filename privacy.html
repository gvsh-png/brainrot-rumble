Brainrot Swarm Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

Brainrot Swarm is a mobile game. This policy explains what data the game stores and how it is used.

Data stored on your device

Whether you play as a guest or sign in, the game stores progress locally on your device, including:

  • Game progress (worlds unlocked, gold, gems, gear, characters, pets)
  • Achievements, Swarm Rank, daily bounty progress, and Boss Rush records
  • Audio and graphics settings
  • Suspended run data if you quit mid-battle (device only)

Google Play Games cloud save (Android only)

If you sign in with Google Play Games in the Android app, a snapshot of your profile progress is saved to Google Play Games Saved Games so you can restore progress on another Android device signed into the same Play Games account.

Cloud save includes progression data such as gold, worlds, gear, gems, pets, achievements, and Boss Rush stats. Continue-run saves and device settings are not synced.

What we do not collect directly

The game does not include its own account server. We do not receive your email address or password through this game client.

Google Play Games and Google Play Console may process data according to Google’s policies when you use sign-in or download the app from Google Play.

Children

The game is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through Google Play, contact Google or the developer through the Play Store listing.

Changes

This policy may be updated when save features or store requirements change. The in-game Update Log notes major client changes.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent through the Google Play Store developer contact for Brainrot Swarm.
